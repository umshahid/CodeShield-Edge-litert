# Recording Checklist

## Before Recording

- Use the packaged app at `dist/CodeShield Edge.app`.
- Use the synthetic card at `submission/video/assets/synthetic_gift_card.png`.
- Do not show a real unused gift-card code.
- Make the app window large enough that the warning and Gemma JSON are readable.
- Keep the desktop clean or crop tightly to the app. Do not publish full-display screenshots that include private tabs, meetings, faces, or desktop notifications.
- Record at 1080p or higher.
- Target final length: 3 to 3.5 minutes.

## Shot Order

1. Open on the Messages screen.
2. Open Scammer Console and send a scam message.
3. Click `AI Next Message` to show local AI scammer generation.
4. In Messages, reply normally.
5. Attach `synthetic_gift_card.png`.
6. Press send and pause on the large warning.
7. Wait for Gemma JSON to finish.
8. Click `Don't Send`.
9. Switch to Audio Call.
10. Click `Start AI Scam Call`.
11. Let the AI caller speak.
12. Type or say a safe response, then send.
13. Type or say `The PIN is ATF7LQJ4AL9YWFDV.`
14. Send and pause on the audio warning.
15. Wait for Gemma JSON.
16. End on the app or a title card.

## Lines To Say Out Loud

- "The code has not left this Mac."
- "Gemma 4 E4B is running locally through LiteRT-LM."
- "The app warns only when risky context and a redeemable code meet."
- "This can become an OS-level safety layer, but the prototype already works end to end."

## Do Not Include

- A real gift-card code.
- Any private messages.
- Long terminal logs.
- Kaggle/repo setup details in the main video.
